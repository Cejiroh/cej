17. Number and Size of Households:
A household is defined not just by people living under the same roof, but by whether they share food and expenses together.
If everyone in the house shares meals and has one food budget, that counts as one household.
If groups of people in the same house buy, cook, and eat separately, then each group is considered a separate household.

Reminder:
  • All residential should have at least 1 household.
  • All Semi-business should have at least 1 business.
  • Not all residential have 0 business.
  • Semi-business can have 0 household.
  • If any of the field is blank, ask the enumerator for confirmation.
